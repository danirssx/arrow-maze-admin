## Result Obtained

- **Repo bootstrap:** created `develop` from `main`; scaffold on `feat/scaffold-clean-arch-MAZ-201` (worktree `worktrees/am-MAZ-201-admin`); PR → `develop`.
- **Stack/tooling:** Vite 5 + React 18 + TS (strict, `noUncheckedIndexedAccess`,
